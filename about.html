About Village Copa

Village Copa is not your regular skit maker — he's the Corper with vibes. Known for rocking his NYSC khaki with pride and turning everyday Naija experiences into laughter-filled content, Village Copa has carved a name for himself in the comedy space with over 23,000 loyal followers across social media platforms. His skits are a blend of village humor, youth service life, and pure Naija reality, capturing the struggles, surprises, and sweetness of being a young Nigerian. Whether he’s acting as a confused corper in a remote village or dropping wisecracks about community life, his content hits different — because it's real. From relatable NYSC tales to hilarious cultural twists, Village Copa brings village vibes to the global stage, one skit at a time.
